<p><p>The Nuclear Decommissioning Services market encompasses three primary applications for decommissioning nuclear facilities: Immediate Dismantling, Deferred Dismantling, and Entombment. Immediate Dismantling involves rapid removal and disposal of nuclear components to minimize risks promptly. Deferred Dismantling allows for a waiting period, during which radiological decay occurs, making dismantling safer and easier. Entombment involves securing radioactive structures in place, rendering them safe while isolating radiation. Each method is selected based on safety, regulatory requirements, time, and cost considerations.</p></p>
