<p><p>Coating machines for the pharmaceutical market come in various types, each serving distinct purposes. Standard coating pans are used for uniform coating of tablets and pellets. Perforated coating pans utilize perforated surfaces for improved air circulation, enhancing drying efficiency. Fluidized bed/air suspension coaters apply coatings in a fluidized bed, allowing for even distribution and improved adhesion. Other market options may include tablet coaters with specialized capabilities, catering to specific formulation needs or production scales, offering versatility in coating processes.</p></p>
