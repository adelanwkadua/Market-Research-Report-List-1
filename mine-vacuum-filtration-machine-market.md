<p><ul><li>Cylindrical (Drum) Filter</li><li>Disc Filter</li><li>Horizontal Belt Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mine vacuum filtration machine market features three primary types: Cylindrical (Drum) Filters, Disc Filters, and Horizontal Belt Filters. Cylindrical filters operate by drawing slurry through a rotating drum, efficiently separating solids from liquids. Disc filters utilize multiple rotating discs to enhance filtration capacity, making them suitable for high-solid-content applications. Horizontal Belt Filters consist of a continuous belt that moves the slurry, enabling effective dewatering and recovery of valuable minerals. Each type offers distinct advantages based on operational needs and specific mining applications.</p></p>
