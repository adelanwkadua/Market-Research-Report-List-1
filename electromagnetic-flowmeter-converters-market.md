<p><ul><li>Integrated Type</li><li>Remote Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electromagnetic flowmeter converters come in two primary types: Integrated and Remote. Integrated types combine both the flow sensor and the electronic converter in a single unit, offering compactness and easier installation, making them ideal for space-constrained environments. Remote types feature a separate sensor and converter, allowing for flexibility in installation and maintenance, particularly in high-temperature or hazardous locations. Both types effectively measure flow by detecting the induced voltage in conductive fluids, ensuring accuracy and reliability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
