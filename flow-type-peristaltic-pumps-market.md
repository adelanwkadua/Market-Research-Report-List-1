<p><p>Flow-type peristaltic pumps are designed to move fluids through a series of elastic tubes by creating peristaltic motion. The market for these pumps can be divided into two main types: fixed speed and variable speed. Fixed speed peristaltic pumps operate at a constant flow rate, making them suitable for applications where consistent delivery is essential. In contrast, variable speed peristaltic pumps provide adjustable flow rates, offering flexibility for processes with varying demands or precise dosing requirements.</p></p>
