<p><p>Moisture barrier fabrics for law enforcement and firefighting are categorized into two main types: breathable and non-breathable. Breathable fabrics allow moisture vapor from perspiration to escape while preventing external water from entering, keeping personnel dry and comfortable during intense activities. Non-breathable fabrics, on the other hand, offer a higher level of water resistance but can trap moisture, potentially leading to discomfort during prolonged use. Each type serves specific needs based on operational requirements and environmental conditions faced by first responders.</p></p>
