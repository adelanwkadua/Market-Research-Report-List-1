<p><p>Ultrafine silica fume, or microsilica, is categorized into three market types based on its density. Densified silica fume has been compacted to reduce volume and increase bulk density, enhancing usability in construction. Semi-densified silica fume retains some air pockets, balancing flow and cohesion. Undensified silica fume is a lightweight, fluffy product that requires careful handling due to its high surface area and dust potential. Each type serves specific applications in concrete, construction, and various industrial processes.</p></p>
