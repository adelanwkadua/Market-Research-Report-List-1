<p><strong>The Laminar Flow Hoods for Laboratories Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminar flow hoods are critical for maintaining contamination-free environments in laboratories. Horizontal flow hoods direct air from the back of the unit towards the user, creating a protective barrier for products and personnel. They are ideal for applications requiring large workspace and easy access. In contrast, vertical flow hoods bring air down from the top, suitable for tasks involving large equipment or when working with hazardous materials. Both types ensure a sterile field by filtering air through HEPA or ULPA filters.</p></p>
