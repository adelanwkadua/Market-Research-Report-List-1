<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Solid</li><li>Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor adhesives market consists of three primary types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives offer excellent flow properties and are used for bonding components in intricate designs. Solid adhesives are typically pre-formed and provide robust, durable bonds, suitable for high-temperature applications. Paste adhesives, often utilized in printing or die attach processes, allow for precise application and alignment of semiconductor devices. Each type caters to specific manufacturing needs, enhancing performance and reliability in semiconductor production.</p></p>
