<p><ul><li>Single Corrugated Cardboard</li><li>Double Corrugated Cardboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated paper market primarily consists of two types: single corrugated cardboard and double corrugated cardboard. Single corrugated cardboard features one layer of fluted medium sandwiched between two linerboards, making it lightweight and suitable for packaging lightweight items. In contrast, double corrugated cardboard consists of two layers of fluted medium, providing enhanced strength and durability, ideal for heavier products or transportation. Both types are widely used in packaging, shipping, and storage applications due to their versatility and protective qualities.</p></p>
