<p><p>The depression medicine market includes several types of medications designed to treat depressive disorders. Tricyclic Antidepressants (TCAs) increase neurotransmitter levels, while Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors (SSRIs) specifically boost serotonin. Serotonin-norepinephrine Inhibitors (SNRIs) enhance both serotonin and norepinephrine. Atypical Antipsychotics provide mood stabilization, whereas Benzodiazepines offer short-term anxiety relief. Tetracyclic Antidepressants and Monoamine Oxidase Inhibitors (MAOIs) target neurotransmitter systems differently. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses alternative treatments like herbal remedies and neuromodulation therapies.</p></p>
