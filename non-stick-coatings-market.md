<p><p>The non-stick coatings market comprises primarily three types: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy), and FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene). PTFE is widely used for cookware due to its excellent non-stick properties and high-temperature resistance. PFA offers more flexibility and is suitable for industrial applications, while FEP provides clarity and good chemical resistance. Each coating type serves distinct purposes across various sectors, including consumer products, aerospace, and automotive, driven by factors like performance, durability, and chemical compatibility.</p></p>
