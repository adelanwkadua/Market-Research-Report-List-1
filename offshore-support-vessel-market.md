<p><p>The Offshore Support Vessel market comprises two main types: Shallow Water and Deepwater vessels. Shallow Water vessels are designed for operations in coastal areas and continental shelves, typically supporting activities like drilling, production, and maintenance in depths up to 500 meters. In contrast, Deepwater vessels operate in more challenging and remote environments, beyond 500 meters, facilitating complex operations such as subsea installations and exploration in harsher conditions. Both types are essential for optimizing offshore oil and gas projects.</p></p>
