<p><strong>The Handheld Laser Power Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Type</li><li>Thermoelectric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld laser power meter market features two main types: photoelectric and thermoelectric. The photoelectric type utilizes photodetectors to convert laser light into an electrical signal, offering high sensitivity and fast response times, ideal for precise measurements. In contrast, the thermoelectric type employs a thermopile sensor that measures heat generated by laser absorption, providing direct readings for higher power levels. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring accurate laser power measurement for diverse applications.</p></p>
