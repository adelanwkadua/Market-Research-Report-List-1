<p><ul><li>CCD Sensor</li><li>CMOS Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intraoral digital cameras market features two main types of sensors: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors provide high-quality images with excellent light sensitivity, making them suitable for detailed dental examinations. In contrast, CMOS sensors are typically smaller, more energy-efficient, and can offer faster image processing, which enhances workflow efficiency. Each sensor type presents distinct advantages, catering to varying preferences in dental practice while contributing to advancements in intraoral imaging technology.</p></p>
