<p><p>The waterborne container coatings market primarily features two types: epoxy and acrylic acid. Epoxy coatings are known for their excellent adhesion, chemical resistance, and durability, making them suitable for various container applications. On the other hand, acrylic acid coatings offer a high gloss finish and good weather resistance, providing flexibility and ease of application. Both types are environmentally friendly alternatives to solvent-based coatings, contributing to lower volatile organic compound emissions and enhancing sustainability in container production.</p></p>
