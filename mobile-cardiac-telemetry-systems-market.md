<p><strong>The Mobile Cardiac Telemetry Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile Cardiac Telemetry Systems are classified into two main types: Single Channel and Multi-Channel. Single Channel systems monitor one lead of heart activity, offering basic data on heart rhythm and arrhythmias, making them suitable for straightforward assessments. In contrast, Multi-Channel systems capture multiple leads simultaneously, providing a comprehensive view of cardiac function. This allows for detailed analysis, facilitating the diagnosis of complex heart conditions. Both types enhance remote monitoring, improving patient care and enabling timely medical interventions.</p></p>
