<p><strong>The Yoghurt Making Culture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mesophilic Type</li><li>Thermophilic Type</li><li>Probiotics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Yogurt making cultures are categorized into mesophilic, thermophilic, and probiotic types. Mesophilic cultures ferment at moderate temperatures, typically between 20-45°C, producing a thicker texture and tangy flavor, suitable for homemade yogurt. Thermophilic cultures thrive in higher temperatures, around 40-45°C, leading to a smooth, creamy consistency and commonly used in commercial yogurt production. Probiotic cultures, rich in beneficial bacteria, not only enhance flavor and texture but also promote gut health, catering to health-conscious consumers seeking functional food options.</p></p>
