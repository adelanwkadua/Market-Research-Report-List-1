<p><p>Brain aneurysm treatment focuses on managing and repairing weakened blood vessels in the brain to prevent rupture, which can lead to severe complications. The primary treatment options include surgical clipping, endovascular coiling, and flow diversion devices. Surgical clipping involves placing a clip on the neck of the aneurysm, while endovascular coiling utilizes coils to induce clotting within the aneurysm. Flow diversion devices redirect blood flow away from the aneurysm, promoting healing.</p><p>The Brain Aneurysm Treatment Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.3% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of brain aneurysms, advancements in medical technology, and the rising awareness of neurological disorders. Additionally, the growing geriatric population and improvements in diagnostic imaging techniques are further propelling market expansion. Recent trends include the development of minimally invasive procedures, enhancing patient recovery times, and the incorporation of AI and robotics in neurosurgery for enhanced precision. Ongoing research and clinical trials are also focused on new therapeutic approaches and devices, promising to shape the future landscape of brain aneurysm treatments.</p></p>
