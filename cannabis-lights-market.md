<p><p>The cannabis lights market encompasses various types of lighting, each suited for different growth stages. LED lights are energy-efficient and versatile, providing tailored spectra for plant growth. High Pressure Sodium (HPS) lights excel in flowering phases, offering intense light output. Metal Halide (MH) lights are ideal for vegetative growth, promoting compact plant structure. UV lights enhance resin production and potency. Fluorescent grow lights are suitable for seedlings and clones due to low heat emission. Ceramic Metal Halide (CMH) lights combine benefits of MH and HPS, offering full-spectrum light.</p></p>
