<p><p>The nanoporous materials market consists of several key types, including zeolites, clays, activated carbon, silica gel, activated alumina, and others. Zeolites are crystalline structures used for molecular sieving and catalysis. Clays provide natural sorption and barrier properties. Activated carbon excels in adsorption applications due to its high surface area. Silica gel serves as a desiccant and moisture control agent. Activated alumina is utilized in adsorption and filtration processes. Other materials include metal-organic frameworks and carbon-based nanoporous materials, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
