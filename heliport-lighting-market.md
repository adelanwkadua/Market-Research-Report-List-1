<p><ul><li>High intensity lights (HIL)</li><li>Medium intensity lights (MIL)</li><li>Low intensity lights (LIL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heliport lighting market comprises three main types based on intensity: High Intensity Lights (HIL), Medium Intensity Lights (MIL), and Low Intensity Lights (LIL). HIL is designed for large, busy heliports, offering high visibility over long distances, suitable for adverse weather conditions. MIL provides balanced illumination for mid-sized operations, ensuring safety without overwhelming brightness. LIL is utilized in smaller, less busy heliports or remote locations, focusing on energy efficiency while still offering essential visibility for landing and takeoff operations.</p></p>
