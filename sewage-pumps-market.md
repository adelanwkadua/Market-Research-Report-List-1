<p><strong>The Sewage Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Day Sewage Pumps</li><li>Submersible Sewage Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sewage pump market primarily comprises two types: day sewage pumps and submersible sewage pumps. Day sewage pumps are typically installed above ground and are used for transferring sewage during daylight hours, with easy maintenance access. In contrast, submersible sewage pumps are submerged in wastewater and feature a sealed motor, allowing them to handle large volumes of sewage efficiently. They are ideal for continuous operation in environments where space is limited and require minimal maintenance intervention.</p></p>
