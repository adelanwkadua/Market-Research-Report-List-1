<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The office software market consists of two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a user's hardware, offering greater control and security but requiring significant maintenance and upfront costs. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access applications via the internet. This model promotes flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT burdens, often featuring subscription-based pricing. Both types meet diverse business needs, each with distinct advantages and trade-offs.</p></p>
