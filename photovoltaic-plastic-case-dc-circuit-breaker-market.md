<p><p>The photovoltaic plastic case DC circuit breaker market is primarily segmented into two types: unipolar ground and ungrounded. Unipolar ground circuit breakers are designed to connect the negative side of the circuit to ground, enhancing safety by preventing overvoltage scenarios. In contrast, ungrounded circuit breakers operate without a direct connection to ground, offering flexibility in systems requiring isolation. Both types ensure reliable performance and protection in solar applications, catering to diverse installation and safety needs within the photovoltaic industry.</p></p>
