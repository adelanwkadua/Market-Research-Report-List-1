<p><p>The targeted biomarker market is categorized into three main types: safety biomarkers, efficacy biomarkers, and validation biomarkers. Safety biomarkers assess the adverse effects and risks associated with treatments, ensuring patient safety during clinical trials. Efficacy biomarkers measure the therapeutic effectiveness of a drug, helping to determine its clinical benefits. Validation biomarkers are used to confirm the reliability and accuracy of other biomarkers, supporting their use in diagnosing conditions and monitoring treatment response. Together, these biomarkers enhance precision medicine efforts.</p></p>
