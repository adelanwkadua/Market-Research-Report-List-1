<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Base Coat</li><li>Clearcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive plastic parts coatings market can be categorized into three primary types: primer, base coat, and clearcoat. Primer is the initial layer that enhances adhesion and ensures corrosion resistance on plastic surfaces. The base coat provides color, while also contributing to visual appeal and durability. Clearcoat serves as the final protective layer, offering gloss and safeguarding against environmental damage, UV rays, and scratches. Together, these coatings ensure the longevity and aesthetic quality of automotive plastic components.</p></p>
