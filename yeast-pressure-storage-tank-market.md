On the other hand, cylindroconical tanks have a conical bottom, enhancing yeast sedimentation and enabling efficient removal of settled yeast. Both designs ensure optimal fermentation processes and maintenance of yeast viability, catering to the needs of breweries and fermentation industries for improved production efficiency.</p></p>
