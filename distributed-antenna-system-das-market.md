<p><ul><li>Active DAS</li><li>Passive DAS</li><li>Hybrid DAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Distributed Antenna System (DAS) enhances cellular coverage and network capacity in buildings or areas where signals are weak. The three main market types are:</p><p>1. **Active DAS**: Utilizes powered, remotely located antennas connected to a central base station, providing superior signal quality and coverage.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Passive DAS**: Employs coaxial cables and antennas without amplification, relying solely on signal distribution, making it cost-effective but with limited range.</p><p>3. **Hybrid DAS**: Combines active and passive elements, optimizing performance and flexibility for various environments.</p></p>
