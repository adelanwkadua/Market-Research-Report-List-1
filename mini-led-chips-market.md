<p><p>The Mini-LED chips market comprises three main types: Lateral Chip LED, Vertical Chip LED, and Flip Chip LED. Lateral Chip LED features lateral light emission, enhancing design flexibility and efficiency. Vertical Chip LED allows for a compact structure, improving thermal management and brightness. Flip Chip LED offers superior performance with direct electrical connections, resulting in reduced light loss and enhanced reliability. Each type caters to specific display applications, driving advancements in technology and market growth in various sectors.</p></p>
