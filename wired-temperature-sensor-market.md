<p><p>The wired temperature sensor market is categorized into One Wire, Two Wire, Three Wire, and Others. One Wire sensors utilize a single wire for both power and data, simplifying installation. Two Wire sensors have separate lines for power and signal, offering better accuracy and reduced noise. Three Wire sensors provide improved precision by compensating for the resistance in the wires. The "Others" category includes specialized or emerging technologies that blend these configurations, catering to diverse industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
