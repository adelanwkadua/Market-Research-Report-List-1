<p><p>The lentiviral vectors market is categorized into three generations, each characterized by advancements in safety and efficacy. </p><p>1st-generation vectors, while effective, posed safety concerns due to a lack of regulatory elements. </p><p>2nd-generation vectors improved upon this with enhanced safety features by removing some viral genes, reducing the risk of insertional mutagenesis. </p><p>3rd-generation vectors offer even greater safety and versatility, utilizing stable packaging systems and often incorporating additional regulatory sequences. These advancements enhance their application in gene therapy and research.</p></p>
