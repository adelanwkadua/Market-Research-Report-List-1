<p><p>The commercial dishwasher market consists of three primary types: Undercounter Dishwashers, Conveyor Dishwashers, and Door-type Dishwashers. Undercounter models are compact and designed for small establishments, fitting beneath countertops for space efficiency. Conveyor dishwashers are ideal for high-volume operations, utilizing a continuous belt system to streamline the washing process. Door-type dishwashers combine efficiency and accessibility, featuring a front-loaded door for easy loading and unloading, making them suitable for mid-sized restaurants and catering operations. Each type caters to different operational needs.</p></p>
