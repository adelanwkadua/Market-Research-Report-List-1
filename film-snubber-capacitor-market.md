<p><p>The film snubber capacitor market includes two primary types: single metalized and double metalized snubber capacitors. Single metalized snubber capacitors feature a single layer of metalization, offering basic performance and cost efficiency for applications with moderate demands. In contrast, double metalized snubber capacitors are designed with two layers of metalization, providing enhanced performance capabilities, improved thermal stability, and better energy dissipation. Both types are crucial for suppressing voltage spikes and improving the reliability of power electronic systems.</p></p>
