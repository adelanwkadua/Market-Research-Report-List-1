<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Diesel Fuel Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System</li><li>Common Rail Injection System</li><li>Other Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive diesel fuel injection system market comprises several types, including the Pump-Line-Nozzle (PLN) injection system, Common Rail injection system, and other injection systems. The PLN system utilizes a dedicated pump for each cylinder, delivering high pressure directly to the nozzle, ensuring precise fuel delivery. The Common Rail system features a single high-pressure rail supplying multiple injectors, allowing for better fuel atomization and efficiency. Other injection systems may include advanced technologies like unit injectors or electronic control systems, enhancing performance and emissions control.</p></p>
