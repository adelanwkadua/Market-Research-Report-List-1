<p><p>Stem cell therapy for osteoarthritis involves two primary treatment approaches: monotherapy and combination therapy. In contrast, combination therapy integrates stem cells with additional treatments, such as PRP (platelet-rich plasma) or hyaluronic acid, enhancing overall efficacy and addressing multiple facets of osteoarthritis. Both approaches focus on alleviating symptoms and improving joint function, offering potential benefits for patients seeking alternatives to conventional therapies.</p></p>
